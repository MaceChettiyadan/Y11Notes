#### Linear Motion & Force
##### Vectors & Scalars
- Vectors have a **direction** and a **magnitude**
	- To add, or subtract vectors, we can use the pythagorean theorem
- Scalars only have a **magnitude**

##### SUVAT
- **S** - displacement
	- Speed is the scalar version of displacement.
- **U** - initial velocity
- **V** - final velocity
- **A** - Acceleration
- **T** - Time
- The equations;
	- $v=\frac{s}{t}$
	- $v^2=u^2+2as$
	- $s=ut+\frac{1}{2}at^2$
	- $a=\frac{v-u}{t}$
	- $v=u+at$
